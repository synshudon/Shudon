ABOUT

Shudon Board Game Club is the best space for board game enthusiasts, offering an unmatched collection of 500+ board games, and a unique space for you to spend time and engage with friends, family or colleagues. Why buy board games when you can come to Shudon?

You can also make new friends here, as we aim to create an open, welcoming environment and help in the development of a board game community.

Previous Next

Our vast collection of 500+ board games includes old classics like Monopoly and Scrabble, to some of the newer popular games like Shasn, or those that only the OGs would know like On Mars, It's A Wonderful World, Smartphone Inc., or Rising Sun. The collection spans various genres like party games, family games, war games, thematic games and even abstract games. You can play short, fun 15 minute games or play intense, world building games that go on for 7 hours (and everything in between).
